# PERFORMANCE NOTES

## Volume Swell Technique
- Essential for this tune
- Roll volume knob up slowly
- Let notes bloom, then decay naturally
- Practice: one note per breath

## Space as Composition
- Silence is as important as notes
- Don't fill every bar
- Let chords speak
- Rubato throughout

## Wide Voicings
- High positions for clarity
- Let strings ring
- Campanella effect where possible
- Minimal left-hand movement

## Emotional Arc
- Start quiet, stay quiet
- Climax is textural, not loud
- End with question, not answer
