Non-Functional Characteristics

1. No V-I cadences — The harmony never resolves functionally 2. Chromatic root motion — Roots move by half-step, not by fifth 3. Major 7th predominance — Almost all chords are maj7 variants 4. Lydian as default — The ♯11 creates floating, unresolved quality 5. Color over function — Chords relate by sound color, not tension-release

Wayne Shorter Influence

This progression emulates Shorter's approach from albums like: - *Speak No Evil* (1964) - *JuJu* (1964) - *Nefertiti* (1967)

Key Shorter characteristics present: - Through-composed form - Modal interchange without functional resolution - Mysterious, floating quality - Wide dynamic range implied by sparse texture
