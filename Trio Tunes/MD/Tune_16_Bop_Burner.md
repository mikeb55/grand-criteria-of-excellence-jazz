## 2. SCALE PALETTE STUDY

*   **Primary Universe (FMaj7):** F Ionian / F Bebop Major (add E-Eb passing tone).
*   **ii-V (Gm7-C7):** G Dorian → C Mixolydian / C Bebop Dominant.
*   **Half-Diminished (Em7b5):** E Locrian / E Locrian ♮2.
*   **Secondary Dominant (A7, D7):** Respective Mixolydian / Altered scales.
*   **Borrowed iv (Bbm7):** Bb Dorian.

**Parker/Benson Logic:**
*   **Bebop scale = 8 notes** — add chromatic passing tone for even division.
*   **Enclosures are mandatory** — surround target notes.
*   **Swing hard** — even at 240 BPM, the "and" of beats should be slightly early.
*   **Digital patterns** — 1-2-3-5, 3-5-7-9, etc.

## 4. THREE ETUDE CHORUSES

### **Chorus 1: Parker Heads (Melody)**
*   **Concept:** Classic bebop melody with characteristic leaps and enclosures.

*   **Bars 5-8:** Sequence the motif over ii-V.
*   **Bars 17-24 (B):** Contrast — more stepwise motion.

*   **Concept:** Maximum chromaticism — every other note is a passing tone.
