## 4. THREE ETUDE CHORUSES

### **Chorus 1: Lyrical Melody (Bebop Language)**
*   **Concept:** "Omnibook" phrasing.
*   **Motif:** Charlie Parker-esque licks (triplets, chromatic passing tones).
*   **Goal:** Authentic bebop vocabulary.

### **Chorus 2: Modern Triad-Pair Lines**
*   **Concept:** George Benson Picking.
*   **Pattern:** Rapid triplet bursts using triad pairs.
*   **Technique:** Locked-hand picking technique.

### **Chorus 3: Wyble Two-Voice Counterpoint**
*   **Concept:** Walking Bass + Jabs.
*   **Texture:**
    *   *Bottom Voice:* Fast walking bass (quarter notes).
    *   *Top Voice:* Syncopated chord stabs on the "and" of 2 and 4.
*   **Challenge:** Maintaining the burning tempo.
