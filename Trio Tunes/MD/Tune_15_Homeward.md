## 2. SCALE PALETTE STUDY

*   **Primary Universe (D Major):** D Major Pentatonic.
*   **Soul Touch (D7/C):** D Mixolydian.
*   **Minor Plagal (Gm/Bb):** D Minor / G Dorian.
*   **Technique:** Sliding into major 3rds.

**Barry Harris Logic:**
*   Use **chromatic passing chords** (diminished) between the I and ii chords (D - D#dim - Em).

## 4. THREE ETUDE CHORUSES

### **Chorus 1: Lyrical Melody (Double Stops)**
*   **Concept:** Harmonized Melody.
*   **Technique:** Harmonizing the melody in 6ths on the top two strings.
*   **Goal:** Imitating a pedal steel guitar.

### **Chorus 2: Modern Triad-Pair Lines**
*   **Concept:** "Country Jazz."
*   **Pattern:** Enclosing the major 3rd of each chord.
*   **Rhythm:** Laid-back 16th notes.

### **Chorus 3: Wyble Two-Voice Counterpoint**
*   **Concept:** Chorale Style.
*   **Texture:**
    *   *Bottom Voice:* Root motion (Bass).
    *   *Top Voice:* Moving inner voices.
*   **Result:** A self-contained hymn.
