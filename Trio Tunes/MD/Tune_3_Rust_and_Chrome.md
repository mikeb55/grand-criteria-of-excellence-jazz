## 2. SCALE PALETTE STUDY

*   **Primary Universe (E7#9):** E Blues Scale / E Super Locrian (for outside tension).
*   **Funk Color (A13):** A Mixolydian.
*   **Lydian Shift (Cmaj7, Bbmaj7):** Lydian colors planing down.
*   **Slash Chords:** Mixolydian Sus sounds (G/A = A11).

**Barry Harris Logic:**
*   On **E7#9**, use **F Diminished** (Half-Step Above) to create the "7b9" tension resolving to E.

## 4. THREE ETUDE CHORUSES

### **Chorus 1: Lyrical Melody (Angular)**
*   **Concept:** Sco-style articulation (hammer-ons from nowhere).
*   **Motif:** E -> G (Hammer) -> Bb (Slide).
*   **Rhythm:** Heavily syncopated 16th notes.

### **Chorus 2: Modern Triad-Pair Lines**
*   **Concept:** Combining E Major and G Major triads over the static E7 groove.
*   **Technique:** Double stops (3rds and 4ths) moving in parallel.

### **Chorus 3: Wyble Two-Voice Counterpoint**
*   **Concept:** Funky counterpoint.
*   **Texture:**
    *   *Bottom Voice:* Muted E string bass line (Root-b7).
    *   *Top Voice:* Staccato double stops on beats 2 and 4.
*   **Challenge:** Maintaining the groove while separating the voices.
