## 2. SCALE PALETTE STUDY

*   **Primary Universe (Db7):** Db Whole Tone (Db-Eb-F-G-A-B).
*   **Chromatic Planning:** Dominant 7th chords moving in parallel half-steps.
*   **Rhythm:** Quarter note triplets and displaced accents.

**Barry Harris Logic:**
*   **Dominant Diminished** scales apply to every dominant chord, but the Whole Tone scale is the "Monk" flavor.

## 4. THREE ETUDE CHORUSES

### **Chorus 1: Lyrical Melody (Angular)**
*   **Concept:** "Ugly Beauty."
*   **Motif:** Wide intervals (Minor 9ths) played softly.
*   **Phrasing:** Leaving lots of space.

### **Chorus 2: Modern Triad-Pair Lines**
*   **Concept:** Augmented Chain.
*   **Pattern:** Linking augmented triads in whole steps.
*   **Effect:** A floating, unsettled sound.

### **Chorus 3: Wyble Two-Voice Counterpoint**
*   **Concept:** Stride Piano Simulation.
*   **Texture:**
    *   *Bottom Voice:* Root-7 shell voicings on beats 1 and 3.
    *   *Top Voice:* Melody and chord tones on beats 2 and 4.
*   **Challenge:** Keeping the swing feel heavy and deliberate.
