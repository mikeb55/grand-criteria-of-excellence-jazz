## 2. SCALE PALETTE STUDY

*   **Primary Universe (Gbmaj7):** Gb Ionian / Bebop Major.
*   **Bridge (Bb7-Eb7-Ab7-Db7):** Dominant Bebop Scales moving through the Cycle of 5ths.
*   **Angular Concept:** Avoid step-wise motion; focus on 3rds, 6ths, and 7ths.

**Barry Harris Logic:**
*   Use the **Dominant Diminished** scale on every dominant chord in the bridge to create "Bebop Gravity."

## 4. THREE ETUDE CHORUSES

### **Chorus 1: Lyrical Melody (Angular)**
*   **Concept:** "Pointillism."
*   **Motif:** Short, staccato jabs on the up-beats.
*   **Contour:** Jagged; no smooth lines.

### **Chorus 2: Modern Triad-Pair Lines**
*   **Concept:** Displacement.
*   **Pattern:** Playing triad pairs in groups of 3 over 4/4 time (Poly-metric).
*   **Goal:** Creating rhythmic tension against the fast pulse.

### **Chorus 3: Wyble Two-Voice Counterpoint**
*   **Concept:** Fast Walking.
*   **Texture:**
    *   *Bottom Voice:* Walking bass line (Gb-G-Ab-A-Bb...).
    *   *Top Voice:* Guide tones (3rds and 7ths) punctuating the rhythm.
*   **Challenge:** Endurance at 200 BPM.
