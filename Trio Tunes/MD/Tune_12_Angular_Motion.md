## 2. SCALE PALETTE STUDY

*   **Primary Universe (GbMaj7):** Gb Lydian or Ionian — emphasis on wide intervals.
*   **ii-V Chains (Abm7-Db7):** Ab Dorian → Db Mixolydian with bebop passing tones.
*   **Tritone ii-V (Cm7b5-F7):** C Locrian → F Altered for maximum tension.
*   **Bebop Scales:** Add chromatic passing tone between 8 and b7 on all dominants.

**Bebop Logic:**
*   Use wide interval skips (6ths, 7ths, octaves) to create the "angular" feel.
*   At 200 BPM, economy picking (sweep + alternate) is mandatory.
*   Target chord tones on downbeats; approach with chromatic enclosures.

## 4. THREE ETUDE CHORUSES

### **Chorus A: Lyrical Melody (Bebop Heads)**
*   **Concept:** Classic bebop head with angular melodic leaps.
*   **Motif:** Wide 6th intervals alternating with chromatic enclosures.
*   **Bars 1-8:** Establish the angular theme over I-iii-ii-V.
*   **Bars 9-16:** Tritone ii-V adds tension; resolve to I.
*   **Bridge (25-32):** B section uses descending sequences.

### **Chorus B: Modern Triad-Pair Lines**
*   **Concept:** Continuous 8th notes at 200 BPM using stacked triads.
*   **Bar 1-2 (GbMaj7):** Gb/Ab pair in 6ths — angular arpeggio.
*   **Bar 5-6 (Abm7-Db7):** Ab minor to Cb Major pair.
*   **Bar 9-10 (Cm7b5-F7):** C diminished to B Major (tritone sub).
*   **Goal:** Bebop lines built from triad superimposition.

### **Chorus C: Wyble Two-Voice Counterpoint**
*   **Concept:** Walking bass line against angular melody.
*   **Texture:**
    *   *Top Voice:* Wide-interval melody with bebop phrasing.
    *   *Bottom Voice:* Quarter-note walking bass through changes.
*   **Key Moment (Bars 9-10):** Top holds high tension; bass walks chromatic.
*   **Ending:** Both voices land on Gb octave unison.

### **Chorus D: Hybrid Synthesis**
*   **Concept:** Combines bebop heads, triad-pair runs, and walking bass.
*   **Bars 1-8:** Bebop head (Chorus A) with sparse bass.
*   **Bars 9-16:** Triad-pair 8ths (Chorus B) at full tempo.
*   **Bridge:** Full counterpoint (Chorus C) with walking bass.
*   **Dynamic Arc:** f throughout — bebop energy.
