## 4. THREE ETUDE CHORUSES

### **Chorus 1: Lyrical Melody (Folk/Americana)**
*   **Concept:** Singable, diatonic melody.
*   **Motif:** Triadic shapes (D-F#-A) shifting over the changing bass notes.
*   **Articulation:** Legato slides into the target notes.

### **Chorus 2: Modern Triad-Pair Lines**
*   **Concept:** "Running" the pairs.
*   **Pattern:** 1-2-3-5 of Triad A -> 5-3-2-1 of Triad B.
*   **Goal:** Seamless connection; no breaks.

### **Chorus 3: Wyble Two-Voice Counterpoint**
*   **Concept:** Pedal tone ostinato.
*   **Texture:**
    *   *Bottom Voice:* Constant pedal A (open string).
    *   *Top Voice:* Moving triads harmonized in 6ths.
*   **Effect:** shimmering, open sound.

## 5. OPTIONAL GUITAR VOICING SET (Metheny Open Strings)
