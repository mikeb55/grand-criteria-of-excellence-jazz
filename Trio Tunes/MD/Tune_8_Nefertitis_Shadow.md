## 4. THREE ETUDE CHORUSES

### **Chorus 1: Lyrical Melody (Sustained)**
*   **Concept:** "Nefertiti" style - the melody repeats, the chords change underneath.
*   **Motif:** G - F - Eb - D descending line.
*   **Rhythm:** Half notes and dotted halves.

### **Chorus 2: Modern Triad-Pair Lines**
*   **Concept:** Searching for the #11.
*   **Technique:** Wide interval skips (6ths and 7ths) using the Lydian pairs.

### **Chorus 3: Wyble Two-Voice Counterpoint**
*   **Concept:** Contrary motion closing intervals.
*   **Texture:**
    *   *Bottom Voice:* Ascending chromatic bass line (Ab-A-Bb-B-C).
    *   *Top Voice:* Descending melodic line.
*   **Result:** Harmonic compression.
