## 2. SCALE PALETTE STUDY

*   **Primary Universe (C7):** C Whole Tone — pure Monk color, symmetric and floating.
*   **Chromatic Approach (Db7):** Db Lydian Dominant — the classic Monk half-step slide.
*   **Diminished Bridge (F#dim7):** F# Octatonic (Half-Whole) — symmetric tension.
*   **Lydian Dominant (G7#11):** G Lydian Dominant — #11 adds the Monk "wrongness."

**Monk Logic:**
*   Use whole-tone clusters (C-D-E-F#-G#-Bb) for color splashes.
*   Displace accents — play on beat 4 instead of beat 1.
*   Leave space — silence is as important as the notes.
*   The Db7 in bar 3 is the "Monk move" — embrace the clash.

## 4. THREE ETUDE CHORUSES

### **Chorus A: Lyrical Melody (Sparse, Monk Style)**
*   **Concept:** Angular melody with intentional silences and rhythmic displacement.
*   **Motif:** Ascending whole-tone (C-D-E) followed by rest, then answer (F#-G#-Bb).
*   **Bars 1-4:** Establish the angular theme with long rests between phrases.
*   **Bars 5-8:** Same shape over IV-#IVdim, with displaced accents.
*   **Bars 9-12:** Turnaround uses Db7 clash; final phrase lands on beat 4.

### **Chorus B: Modern Triad-Pair Lines**
*   **Concept:** Whole-tone triad pairs in continuous 8ths, then sudden stops.
*   **Bar 1-2 (C7):** C Major ascending, D Major descending — whole-tone cascade.
*   **Bar 3 (Db7):** Db Major to Eb Major — Monk slide.
*   **Bar 6 (F#dim7):** F# diminished to A diminished arpeggios.
*   **Bar 10 (G7#11):** G to A triads — Lydian Dominant crunch.
*   **Goal:** Angular lines with sudden silences (Monk humor).

### **Chorus C: Wyble Two-Voice Counterpoint**
*   **Concept:** Low cluster voicings against single-note melody.
*   **Texture:**
    *   *Bottom Voice:* Whole-tone clusters (C-D-E held together) as sustained pads.
    *   *Top Voice:* Sparse, angular melody with displaced accents.
*   **Key Moment (Bar 3 - Db7):** Low Db-Eb-F cluster under high Ab melody.
*   **Key Moment (Bar 6 - F#dim7):** Diminished cluster (F#-A-C) under Eb melody.
*   **Ending:** Cluster collapses to unison C.

### **Chorus D: Hybrid Synthesis**
*   **Concept:** Combines sparse melody, cluster voicings, and sudden triad bursts.
*   **Bars 1-4:** Sparse melody (Chorus A) with cluster bass notes.
*   **Bars 5-8:** Triad-pair burst (Chorus B) interrupts the sparse texture.
*   **Bars 9-12:** Full cluster counterpoint (Chorus C) builds to climax.
*   **Dynamic Arc:** pp with sudden ff stabs → mp → f → sudden silence.

## 6. MONK RHYTHM GUIDE

*   **Displaced Accents:** Play phrases starting on beat 4 or the "and" of 2.
*   **Silence:** Leave at least 1 beat of rest between phrases.
*   **Stabs:** Sudden loud notes after silence create Monk humor.
*   **Clusters:** Let whole-tone clusters ring and decay naturally.
