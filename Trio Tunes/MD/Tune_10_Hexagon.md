## 4. THREE ETUDE CHORUSES

### **Chorus 1: Lyrical Melody (Odd Meter Phrasing)**
*   **Concept:** Phrasing across the bar line to hide the "one."
*   **Motif:** A recurring rhythmic cell (Quarter + Dotted Quarter) that shifts position.
*   **Goal:** Making 5/4 feel like a long 4 or a waltz.

### **Chorus 2: Modern Triad-Pair Lines**
*   **Concept:** Hexatonic Superimposition.
*   **Pattern:** Using the A Major triad over Bm and Gmaj7 to link the chords.
*   **Rhythm:** Continuous 8th notes to establish the grid.

### **Chorus 3: Wyble Two-Voice Counterpoint**
*   **Concept:** 5/4 Ostinato.
*   **Texture:**
    *   *Bottom Voice:* B - F# - B (1-5-8) ostinato pattern (3+2).
    *   *Top Voice:* Melody floating on top.
*   **Challenge:** Maintaining the odd meter groove in the bass.
