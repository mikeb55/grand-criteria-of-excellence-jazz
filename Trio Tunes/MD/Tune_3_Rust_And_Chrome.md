## 2. SCALE PALETTE STUDY

*   **Primary Universe (E7#9):** E Mixolydian with #9 (blues 3rd) — the Hendrix/Scofield hybrid.
*   **Blues Inflection:** E Minor Pentatonic superimposed for grit.
*   **Chromatic Pools (G7, C7):** Use Lydian Dominant for unexpected brightness over dark chords.
*   **Turnaround Tension (B7#9):** B Altered or Half-Whole Diminished for maximum crunch.

**Scofield Logic:**
*   Let the open low E string drone throughout — it anchors the grit.
*   Use slides between frets 2-4 and 5-7 to create the "vocal" quality.
*   Bars 11-12 (C7→B7) is the money moment — dig in hard here.

## 4. THREE ETUDE CHORUSES

### **Chorus A: Lyrical Melody (Groove-Based)**
*   **Concept:** Sparse, rhythmic melody that locks with the funk groove.
*   **Motif:** Syncopated 16th-note pickup into sustained chord tones.
*   **Bars 1-4:** Establish the E7#9 groove with call-and-response phrases.
*   **Bars 5-8:** G7 brings unexpected brightness; let the melody hang.
*   **Bars 9-12:** Build intensity toward C7-B7 turnaround.
*   **Bars 13-16:** Return to opening motif with variation.

### **Chorus B: Modern Triad-Pair Lines**
*   **Concept:** Continuous 16th notes using E/G triad pairs.
*   **Bar 1-2 (E7#9):** E Major triad ascending, G Major descending — the #9 crunch.
*   **Bar 6 (G7):** G Major ascending, F Major descending — Lydian Dominant cascade.
*   **Bar 11-12 (C7→B7):** C Major into D Major for maximum tension, resolve to E.
*   **Goal:** Funky, angular 16th-note lines with accent displacement.

### **Chorus C: Wyble Two-Voice Counterpoint**
*   **Concept:** Open E string pedal against moving melody.
*   **Texture:**
    *   *Bottom Voice:* Open E string (or low E octave) on beats 1 and 3.
    *   *Top Voice:* Syncopated melody on the "and" of beats, sliding between notes.
*   **Key Moment (Bar 6 - G7):** Bass walks E-F-F#-G while melody sustains D.
*   **Key Moment (Bar 11-12):** Bass chromatic descent C-B-Bb-A-G#-E while melody ascends.
*   **Ending:** Low E and high E converge on unison octave.

### **Chorus D: Hybrid Synthesis**
*   **Concept:** Funk groove foundation with triad-pair bursts and contrapuntal moments.
*   **Bars 1-4:** Sparse groove (Chorus A) with open E drone.
*   **Bars 5-8:** Triad-pair 16th runs (Chorus B) over the bIII.
*   **Bars 9-12:** Full two-voice texture (Chorus C) builds to climax.
*   **Bars 13-16:** Return to groove, final unison E.
