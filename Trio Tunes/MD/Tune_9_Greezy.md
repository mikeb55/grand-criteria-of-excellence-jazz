## 2. SCALE PALETTE STUDY

*   **Primary Universe (G7):** G Mixolydian Blues (Major 3rd + b7 + b3 passing).
*   **IV Chord (C7):** C Mixolydian.
*   **Diminished Connection (C#dim7):** C# Whole-Half Diminished (functions as A7b9 to Dm, or back to G/D).
*   **Turnaround (E7alt):** E Super Locrian.

**Barry Harris Logic:**
*   On **G7**, use **G Dominant Diminished** (G7 + F#dim7 shapes).
*   This creates the "Bebop Dominant" scale automatically.

## 4. THREE ETUDE CHORUSES

### **Chorus 1: Lyrical Melody (Shuffle)**
*   **Concept:** The Shuffle Feel.
*   **Motif:** Triplet figures (Swing 8ths) emphasizing the "let" of the triplet.
*   **Phrasing:** Call and response between bars 1-2 and 3-4.

### **Chorus 2: Modern Triad-Pair Lines**
*   **Concept:** Double-Time feel (16th notes in 12/8).
*   **Pattern:** Running the G/F triad pair in continuous 16th note triplets.
*   **Effect:** A burst of energy over the shuffle groove.

### **Chorus 3: Wyble Two-Voice Counterpoint**
*   **Concept:** Walking Bass + Comping.
*   **Texture:**
    *   *Bottom Voice:* Walking bass line on all 4 beats (dotted quarter pulse).
    *   *Top Voice:* Stabs on the "and" of beats (shuffle off-beats).
*   **Challenge:** Independent rhythmic grids.
