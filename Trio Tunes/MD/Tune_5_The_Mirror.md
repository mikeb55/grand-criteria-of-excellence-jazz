## 2. SCALE PALETTE STUDY

*   **Primary Universe (Abmaj9):** Ab Ionian / Ab Lydian (for #4 color).
*   **Secondary Universe (Fm9):** F Dorian (natural 6th brightness).
*   **Modal Interchange (Gbmaj7):** Gb Lydian or Ab Dorian (borrowed from Ab minor).
*   **Bridge Distant Relation (Emaj7):** E Lydian (floating, ethereal quality).
*   **Altered Dominant (Eb7alt):** Eb Super Locrian / Altered Scale.

**Scofield Logic:**
*   **Wide intervals** create tension; let notes decay naturally.
*   On **Bmaj7#11** (Bar 9), use the B Lydian scale to emphasize the #11 (E#/F).
*   The **Dbm(maj7)** in Bar 15 creates a "melancholy turn" — use melodic minor ascending.

## 4. THREE ETUDE CHORUSES

### **Chorus 1: Lyrical Melody (Atmospheric)**
*   **Concept:** Sparse, breathing phrases with maximum sustain and dynamic control.
*   **Motif:** Descending perfect 5th (Eb → Ab) answered by ascending minor 6th.
*   **Bars 1-4:** Whole notes and half notes, minimal movement. Volume swells into each phrase.
*   **Bars 5-8:** Slight rhythmic activation with dotted quarters; maintain space.
*   **Bridge (9-12):** Ascending major 7th leap (G# → G) creates ethereal tension.
*   **Bars 13-16:** Return to opening material; fade on final Ab pedal.

### **Chorus 2: Modern Wide-Interval Lines**
*   **Concept:** Scofield-style intervallic lines; 7ths, 9ths, and tritones.
*   **Bar 1 (Abmaj9):** Eb (top) down a major 9th to Db, then up minor 10th to F.
*   **Bar 3 (Dbmaj7#11):** Ascending tritone (G → Db) into descending major 7th.
*   **Bridge (Emaj7):** Wide arpeggiation (E-G#-B-D#) across 3 octaves.
*   **Bar 12 (Eb7alt):** Chromatic upper neighbor approach: F → Gb → F → Eb.
*   **Goal:** Angular but controlled; use space between phrases.

### **Chorus 3: Wyble Two-Voice Reflection**
*   **Concept:** Mirror counterpoint — when one voice ascends, the other descends.
*   **Texture:**
    *   *Top Voice:* Long tones (whole notes) outlining melody.
    *   *Bottom Voice:* Contrary motion quarter notes, often in 10ths or 6ths.
*   **Key Moment (Bar 9 - Bmaj7#11):** Voices cross — top descends below bottom, then returns.
*   **Bar 15 (Dbm(maj7)):** Chromatic contrary motion: Top (C → Cb), Bottom (Ab → A natural).
*   **Ending:** Voices converge on unison Eb, then spread to octave Ab.
