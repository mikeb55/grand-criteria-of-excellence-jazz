## Performance Notes

### Dynamics
- **Bars 1-4:** mp — establish texture
- **Bars 5-6:** cresc. — build to peak
- **Bar 6:** mf — maximum tension
- **Bars 7-12:** dim. — gradual resolution

### Articulation
- **Top voice:** Sustained, singing quality
- **Bottom voice:** Walking quarter notes, connected

### Independence Tips
1. Practice each voice separately first
2. Start at 60% tempo, gradually increase
3. Focus on bar 6 — the "Wyble moment"
4. Let the bottom voice lead harmonically
5. Top voice provides melodic interest
