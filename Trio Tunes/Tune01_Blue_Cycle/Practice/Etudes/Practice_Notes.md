## Technical Challenges

### 1. Blue Note Bends (Bars 1, 5)
- **Challenge:** Bend from Db to D (half-step)
- **Tip:** Use ring finger with support, push toward floor
- **Practice:** Isolate the bend, match pitch to fretted D

### 2. Diminished Approach (Bar 6)
- **Challenge:** Edim7 arpeggio connecting to Bb7
- **Tip:** Think E-G-Bb-Db as one shape
- **Practice:** Memorize the diminished shape, practice chromatic descent

### 3. Bebop Enclosure (Bar 7)
- **Challenge:** Bb-C-D-F then enclosure to Ab
- **Tip:** Target the Ab, approach from above (Bb) and below (G#)
- **Practice:** Drill 1-2-3-5 pattern in all positions

### 4. Altered Dominant Lines (Bars 8, 10)
- **Challenge:** G7alt and F7alt require altered scale thinking
- **Tip:** Use Ab melodic minor over G7alt, Gb melodic minor over F7alt
- **Practice:** Play altered scales over static dominant vamp

1. **Warm-up:** Blues scale in Bb (5 minutes)
2. **Bend practice:** Bars 1, 5 in isolation (3 minutes)
3. **Diminished shapes:** Bar 6 pattern through positions (3 minutes)
4. **Full etude:** Slowly with metronome at 80 BPM (5 minutes)
5. **Tempo build:** Increase 10 BPM per day until 120 (5 minutes)

- **Week 1:** Learn notes, focus on bends

- **Week 3:** Full tempo, add dynamics
