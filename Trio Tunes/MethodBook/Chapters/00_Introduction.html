Welcome to Version 4 of the GCE Jazz Guitar Method Book. This edition reorganizes the 16 original tunes based on compositional quality, educational value, and performance appeal — placing the strongest material first.

The first six chapters feature tunes that have been independently rated for their harmonic sophistication, stylistic authenticity, and trio performance potential. These "Concert Core" tunes represent the best of the collection and are recommended as priority study material.

All tunes include complete analysis, lead sheets, practice exercises, and performance notes for guitar trio (guitar, bass, drums).
